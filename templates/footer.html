Data Journalism Project: How do New Yorkers commute to work?

To see my original data set, please click on this link: NYC.gov_commuting_data_portal

In this project, I present the differing percent of people by borough who commute to work via public or private transporation, bicycling, or walking. I found that the greatest percentage of people use public transportation in [borough], while [borough] holds the greatest percentage of people who use cars, vans, or trucks. These discrepancies suggest that local governments should do more to make public transportation accessible in [type of borough] since there are environmental benefits to reducing the number of motor vehicles on the road.
